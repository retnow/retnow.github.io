About Work

Hello, worms! ✨

I’m Retno.

Powered by peanut butter, groovy jams, alt-milk lattes and runner's high. Collector of hobbies. Sometimes software engineer.

I’m an iOS engineer in Naarm (Melbourne), and I appreciate beautiful experiences: the chew of freshly made sourdough, brewing coffee, the small ceremony of putting a CD into a player and listening to an album start to finish.

I love storytelling too, whether that’s getting overly invested in a fictional character’s journey or sitting around a dinner table with great humans and letting the conversation stretch out. That same pull towards texture, feeling, and coherence shapes the way I build products.

Work

The short version

Software engineer (iOS) and MEng (EEE) graduate, based in Melbourne. I’ve spent the past 8 years across fintech, retail, and consulting, including 5 years at Block working on Cash App and Afterpay. I care about design-led software, clear systems, readable documentation, and the kind of engineering work that makes products feel simple on the surface and well considered underneath.
